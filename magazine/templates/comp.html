COMP THE HARVARD ADVOCATE

Every semester, The Advocate selects new members through a “Comp” process open to all Harvard undergraduates.

Between its seven boards--Art, Business, Design, Features, Fiction, Poetry, Technology--The Advocate provides a home for writers, artists, critics, designers, web designers, dealmakers, and all others with an interest in the arts and letters.

Compers are evaluated exclusively on their ability to discuss, write about, create, design, sell, or code art.

This semester, introductory comp meetings will take place on Thursday, January 29 and Monday, February 2 at 8pm at 21 South Street.

Though our comp is selective, our hope is that all participants will find it an enriching and rewarding experience.
